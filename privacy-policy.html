Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sudoku mobile application (the “App”) for iOS collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Advertising identifiers: Device-level identifiers such as Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) or Identifier for Vendors (IDFV) for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Anonymous usage analytics: Non-personal information about how you interact with the App (e.g., session length, features used, crash data, performance metrics). This data is aggregated and does not directly identify you.
  • Purchase receipts: Transaction receipts and related metadata required to validate in-app purchases (consumable purchases) and to provide you with the items you purchased.

We do not manually collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details within the App.

How We Use Information

  • Advertising: To display and measure ads, including frequency capping, fraud prevention, and performance reporting.
  • Analytics: To understand App usage, improve features, ensure stability, and fix issues.
  • Purchase processing: To validate, fulfill, and support your in-app purchases.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to support App functionality. These services may collect information as described in their own policies.

  • Google AdMob (advertising): collects device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery and measurement. See AdMob’s Privacy Policy: Google Ads & AdMob Policy.
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchases): processes purchase receipts and related metadata to manage consumable purchases. See RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Apple may also process data as part of the App Store and in-app purchase infrastructure. Refer to Apple’s privacy information: Apple Privacy.

Data Retention

We retain anonymous analytics and purchase validation data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. We take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place consistent with applicable laws.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Advertising preferences: On iOS, you can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier in the device settings.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising within the App where available, or via device settings.
  • Access and deletion: Because we do not collect personal contact information within the App, requests may need to be submitted through your device or platform settings. Where applicable, you may contact us as described below.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information within the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy
