Privacy Policy

Introduction

Mindoku is a Sudoku game for iOS designed to deliver a clean, focused puzzle experience. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Anonymous device data: such as OS version and device model.
  • Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA): used to show personalized advertisements where available.
  • Anonymous analytics events: gameplay statistics and crash reports to help improve stability and features.

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name or email address within the app.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate core features of Mindoku:

  • Google AdMob (advertising). See their policy: Google Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchases and subscriptions). See their policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

How We Use the Data

  • To display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.
  • To analyze usage and improve gameplay, features, and stability.
  • To support purchases and restore access to paid content.

Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Access to data is limited to the developer and the services necessary to operate the app.
  • Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are applied to protect data.

Children’s Privacy

Mindoku is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available on this site, and the “Last updated” date will reflect the latest changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@mindoku.app.
